About Me

Artist & Technology lover!

Welcome to my creative space, where art and technology come together to craft unique experiences. With over 20 years of diverse expertise as a music producer, photographer, designer, and artist, I humbly invite you to explore my artistic journey.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of collaborating on meaningful projects with museums, production companies, and renowned artists. My passion for creativity drives me to continually push boundaries, infusing each project with a distinct touch.

I'm excited to share my artistic world with you and explore the boundless possibilities that music, photography, and design offer. Join me on this journey and let's bring your creative vision to life!
